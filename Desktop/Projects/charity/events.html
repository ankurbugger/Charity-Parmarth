MUSKAN

July 20, 2017 by Admin

"We can't help everyone but everyone can help someone" with this perspective "PARMARTH" the social club of Institute of Engineering & Technology Lucknow organise an event named as "MUSKAN". The name itself define its meaning. The events revolves around distribution of clothes to the needy people of almost every age. The clothes distributed are the contribution of old clothes done by college students, alumini, faculties including honourable Vice Chancellor and Director. The event takes place indside the college campus and less fortunate people from local areas including slums come for the collection of clothes and shoes. Thi pleasurable event is organised twice in a year; at first during summer and then in winter. This remarkable event was started on 22 Nov 2015 then this series is in progress and following are the few dates of the same event 22 anov 2015, 4 April 2016, 14 Nov 2016, 30 April 2017, 29 nov 2017 and 12 May 2018.

UDGAM

July 20, 2017 by Admin

UDGAM- The day for which every member of PARMARTH waits impatiently, whether they be our volunteers or our little children. It is THE FOUNDATION DAY of Parmarth-the day when the idea of social work moulded into a solid shape. Every year Parmarth commemorate 6th of September as its foundation day-Udgam. It is well said that "One must always change, renew, rejuvenate itself, otherwise it hardens". It is the day when we observe how far we have walked with our motto of nourishing young minds and how can we push more in this everlasting journey.
Parmarth has seen 3 golden years of its never-ending journey till now. Every year Parmarth has evolved, becoming better and more fruitful Club, and every milestone has been marked by its foundation day-Udgam. 3 years the back club was started by some great hearts with a motto of 'giving'- giving others what we have been gifted with. Parmarth blew its first birthday cake in Udgam-2k16 and since then it never looked back.
